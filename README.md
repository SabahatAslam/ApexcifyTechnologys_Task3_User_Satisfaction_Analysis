### 📝Customized Dataset

The dataset contains **150 rows and 6 columns**. The columns give us information about users's id, age,	gender,	product category,	purchase amount	and satisfaction levels.
The customer satisfaction survey 🧪 has five levels, namely; 😍 Very Satisfied, 🙂 Satisfied, 😐 Neutral, 🙁 Dissatisfied, 😡 Very Dissatisfied.

### 🔑 Key Concepts

The following important data analysis concepts are used in this project:

* **Data Handling:**
  `read_csv()`, `DataFrame`, column selection

* **Categorical Analysis:**
  `value_counts()`, frequency distribution

* **Grouping & Aggregation:**
  `groupby()`, `size()`, `unstack(fill_value=0)`

* **Data Visualization:**
  `barplot()`, `plot(kind="bar", stacked=True)`, `xlabel()`, `ylabel()`, `title()`, `legend()`, `xticks()`

* **Bivariate Analysis:**
  Comparing two variables (e.g., age vs satisfaction)

### ⚙️Project Objectives
- Organize and explore sales data for all 12 months
- Visualize sales trends using line charts with Matplotlib
- Compare monthly sales performance using bar charts
- Identify highest and lowest sales months
- Analyze overall sales trends and seasonal patterns
- Highlight monthly fluctuations and growth patterns
- Present insights through clear and labeled visualizations
- Help interpret business performance over a yearly period
